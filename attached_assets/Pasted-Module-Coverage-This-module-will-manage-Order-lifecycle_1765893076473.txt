Module Coverage
This module will manage:
•	Order lifecycle
•	Product customization details
•	Inventory availability (via integration)
•	Invoicing
•	Payment collection & proof
•	Order & payment dashboards
1. Order Management
1.1 Order Creation
•	Manual and system/API-based order creation
•	Auto-generation of unique Order Number
•	Capture:
o	Client/company details
o	Industry type
o	Event type
o	Delivery address
o	Required delivery date
o	Order priority (Normal/Urgent)
1.2 Product & Customization Details
•	Apparel type (T-shirt, hoodie, etc.)
•	Size-wise quantity breakup
•	Color and fabric selection
•	Printing type (DTG, Screen, Sublimation, Embroidery)
•	Print placement details
•	Upload design/artwork files
•	Special instructions
2. Inventory Integration (Existing Module)
•	Fetch live stock-in-hand by product, size, and color
•	Stock availability validation at order confirmation
•	Auto-reserve stock
•	Auto-deduct stock at production or dispatch
•	Revert stock on cancellation
•	Display stock status indicators
(Inventory logic remains in the existing module)
3. Order Workflow & Tracking
Configurable order stages:
1.	Order Received
2.	Design Review
3.	Client Approval
4.	Production Scheduled
5.	Printing In Progress
6.	Quality Check
7.	Packed
8.	Dispatched
9.	Delivered
10.	Closed / Cancelled
•	Status change timestamps
•	Delay & cancellation reason capture
•	Order tracking via Order Number
4. Pricing & Commercials
•	Capture:
o	Product cost
o	Printing cost
o	Design/setup charges
o	Discounts
o	Taxes (GST/VAT)
•	Auto-calculation of:
o	Subtotal
o	Tax
o	Grand total
•	Role-based pricing edit permissions
5. Invoice Management
•	Generate Proforma Invoice and Tax Invoice
•	Auto-generated Invoice Number
•	Invoice creation triggers:
o	On order confirmation or dispatch (configurable)
•	Itemized invoice with tax breakup
•	Invoice download & email
•	Invoice version control and revisions
 
6. Payment Tracking & Collection
6.1 Payment Structure
•	Support for:
o	Advance payments
o	Partial payments
o	Full payments
•	Configurable payment milestones
6.2 Payment Capture
For each payment:
•	Payment date
•	Amount received
•	Mode of payment:
o	Cash
o	UPI
o	Bank Transfer
o	Card
o	Cheque
o	Online Gateway
•	Transaction / reference number
•	Upload proof of payment (image or PDF)
•	Remarks
•	User who recorded payment
6.3 Payment Status
•	Auto-calculated:
o	Total invoiced amount
o	Amount received
o	Balance pending
•	Payment status:
o	Not Paid
o	Advance Received
o	Partially Paid
o	Fully Paid
o	Overdue
•	Optional rule to block dispatch if payment pending
 
7. Dashboards & Reporting
7.1 Order Dashboard
•	Orders by status
•	Priority & delayed orders
•	Orders by client, industry, date range
7.2 Payment Dashboard
•	Total invoiced vs collected
•	Pending & overdue payments
•	Advance vs balance payments
•	Mode-wise collection summary
7.3 Management Dashboard
•	Order-to-cash pipeline
•	Revenue trends
•	Client-wise outstanding
•	Aging analysis (0–15, 16–30, 31–60 days)
8. Notifications & Alerts
•	Order status changes
•	Payment received confirmations
•	Pending & overdue payment reminders
•	Dispatch block alerts due to payment issues
9. User Roles & Access Control
•	Sales: Order creation & tracking
•	Accounts: Invoices & payments
•	Production: Order & inventory view
•	Admin: Full access & configuration
•	Management: Dashboard & reports (read-only)
10. Audit & Security
•	Audit trail for:
o	Order updates
o	Invoice changes
o	Payment edits
•	Secure file storage for designs & payment proofs
•	Role-based access control
